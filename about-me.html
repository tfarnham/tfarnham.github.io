Let's dive a little deeper...

I started programming in BASIC back in middle school, but quickly switched to a LAMP stack to work on web applications. I was motivated to run a personal forum, Flash arcade, and just have a digital playground to experiment with.

I received my SB and SM in Mechanical Engineering from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 2014 and 2016, respectively. My research emphasized the importance of the interface between solid, liquid, and gas, and has application to electrical vehicles, hydrate buildup in oil and gas well, and water repellency on fabrics.

Outside of my day job, I enjoy ceramics, baking, and am currently enrolled in an MIT-xPro course on the MERN stack
